## Privacy Policy

InnoCellence Systems Pte. Ltd. considers the protection of personal information to be an important corporate obligation. We have prepared this Privacy Policy, together with our Terms & Conditions, to outline our guidelines and procedures for collecting, storing, and using personal information collected from our website. This Privacy Policy also informs you about your rights regarding the personal information we maintain.

### Acquisition, Use, and Provision of Personal Information

By submitting your details through our website’s Contact Page or using any service that we may provide, you are agreeing to this policy and must read it in its entirety. You also consent to the practices described in this policy, and must not submit your details or use our services unless you accept the policy terms.

### Personal information we collect

“Personal Information” includes any data related to an identified or identifiable person. We obtain personal information from various sources including enquiries submitted through the online form on our Contact Us page, or by communicating with us though phone, post, email, social media, or other means. Personal information is also automatically collected each time you visit the website, as cookies are used by Google Analytics to gather technical and usage data during your visit.

Information we may collect include the following:

- Identity data:  
  may include your first and last name
- Contact data:  
  may include your email address and telephone number
- Technical data:  
  may include your internet protocol (IP) address, location, web browser used, operating system, and other technical details
- Usage data:  
  may include how often you use our website, how long you visit our website, the flow of web pages visited, and other usage details
- Special categories of personal data:  
  may include data related to gender, ethnicity, age, or other personal information in the case of any cover letters and curriculum vitae (CVs) submitted for the purposes of employment
- Other data:  
  we may also collect your contact details when you provide them in the context of becoming a customer, vendor, or partner

### How we may use personal information

We regard the protection of personal information to be a vital part of our business, as it is essential for the successful delivery of services to customers, vendors, and partners. Such services include, but are not limited to the following:

- Planning and construction of websites and other digital content
- Graphic design
- Hosting services
- Consulting

When handling personal information, we will only use the data for purposes that are within the scope of helping our company successfully deliver our services to customers, vendors, and partners. We will only use personal information obtained by other methods only within the scope of their intended purposes, such as using cover letters and CVs submitted for strictly hiring purposes. However, this rule does not apply in instances related to the following situations:

- Situations where we have received the explicit consent of the person in question.
- Situations where we are required to disclose the personal information by law, ordinance, etc.
- Situations where we must disclose personal information to protect the lives, physical well-being, or property of the person in question or the general public.
- Situations where we are required to cooperate with an institution of the central government, a local authority, or a party acting under the charge of an institution of the central government or local authority to carry out an activity provided by law.

### How we may share your personal information

From time to time we may entrust the processing of personal information to a third party in order to improve the efficiency of providing our services to customers, vendors, and partners. In such instances, we will require the third party to control the personal information based on a written agreement or other documentation that’s identical to the guidelines in this Privacy Policy.

### Entrusted Personal Information

From time to time, we may be entrusted with personal information for business purposes. When performing activities to administer personal information, we will prescribe the work contents based on an agreement or other documentation, and use the personal information according to the guidelines within such documentation.

### Proper Control and Safe Retention of Personal Information

We will retain strict control of personal information and keep such details accurate, up- to-date, and within the scope necessary to fulfill its purposes. We will implement appropriate data security measures and corrective actions to prevent unlawful access to, loss, destruction, and falsification of personal information. We will also conduct timely audits to evaluate the effectiveness of our data security measures.

### Observance of Laws, Regulations, Etc.

To ensure compliance with laws, national policies, and other standards regarding the protection of personal information, we will operate a personal information protection management system (PIMS) in accordance with the Japanese Industrial Standards and PIMS requirements. (JIS Q 15001).

### Data Controller

In respect to the personal data of our website users, customers, vendors, and partners, the Data Controller is InnoCellence Systems Pte. Ltd. We will continuously strive to prepare and update rules, policies, and other guidelines to protect personal information.

### Complaints, Consultations, and Subject Access Requests (SARs)

If we receive a complaint or a request for a consultation regarding personal information in our possession from and individual or their representative, we will respond promptly after confirming the identity of the person in question. Under the [General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)](https://gdpr-info.eu/) framework, you have the right to ask us to provide you a copy of your personal data by submitting a Subject Access Request (SAR). We will provide you with the information you request within 30 days once we have verified your identity. Depending on the complexity and number of requests made, it may take us longer to provide you with your personal data. Requests for SARs are free of charge, but we may charge a reasonable fee for time and cost in the following circumstances:

- If an extended amount of time is required to gather and prepare your information
- If you wish to have your information photocopied for you

The GDPR also entitles you to the following rights and protections including:

- The right to be informed of how we will use your data, which is outlined in this Privacy Policy
- The right to ask for your personal information to be updated if you believe our records are inaccurate
- The right to request the deletion of your personal data
- The right to limit how we process your data if there are questions about the data’s accuracy or legitimacy
- The right to request the data you provided to us be returned to you in a machine-readable format, or transmitted to a third-party
- The right to object to us processing your data for our legitimate interests unless we can demonstrate a valid and compelling reason, or the processing is for creation, use, or defense of legal claims All questions, comments, and SAR enquiries should be sent to: [privacy@innocellence.com][privacy@].

### Cookie Policy

Our websites uses cookies for some purposes. "Cookies" are information our web server sends to a visitor's browser. The term refers to the technology that’s used to send and store small amounts of information on a visitor's computer when someone accesses a company’s website. The information stored in the cookie is restricted to information that does not identify the individual, such as the session ID. If you do not wish to accept the cookie, you can change the settings on your browser. In some cases, however, this may prevent you from receiving certain services.

We currently use, or may use the following types of cookies:

- Strictly necessary cookies:  
  These cookies are required for our website to operate, and include cookies that enable you to visit certain areas of our website.
- Analytical/performance cookies:  
  These cookies enable us to recognize and count how many people visit our website, and what pages they view during their visits. They help us improve how our website functions and provides information to visitors.
- Functionality cookies:  
  These are cookies that recognize when you return to our website. They enable us to personalize our content and set preferences such as your choice of language or region.
- Targeting cookies:  
  These cookies record your visit to our website, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We use this information to ensure advertising displayed on our website is more relevant to your interests. We may also share this information with third parties for this purpose.

Please note that third parties (including, for example, advertising networks and providers of external services like web traffic analysis services) may also use cookies, over which we have no control. These cookies are likely to be analytical/performance cookies or targeting cookies.

If you do not wish to have cookies placed on your computer you should set your browsers to refuse cookies before using our website. However, the drawback is that certain features of our website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

### Access Log to This Website

The IP address used when an individual connects to the Internet is recorded when they visit our website. We may use this information to prevent unlawful computer access, respond when such access is detected, and identify and solve the cause of problems that occur with the server. We only use this information to manage and operate our website safely and appropriately, and will not use information related to IP addresses to obtain visitors’ personal information.
